				- Area: 20ft radius
				- Will Partial (half damage and no sicken) DC = 19
		- Daily
			- Power Word Stun

				- Duration: 4d4 rounds if the target has <=50hp, 2d4 rounds if the target has 51-100hp, 1d4 if 101-150
				- Stunned
